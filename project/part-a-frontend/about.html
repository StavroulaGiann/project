About devAcademy

Who we are & what we want to achieve

DevAcademy is a student e-learning platform for Computer Science, designed to resemble a real online education service: courses, books, videos and tools that help the user learn at their own pace.

We offer :

  • Computer Science courses at many levels
  • Library with carefully selected technical and theoritical books
  • Dynamic content search & filtering

The development team

DevAcademy was implemented by a small group of students with a common element of passion for development and a common goal of clean UI and professional results.
